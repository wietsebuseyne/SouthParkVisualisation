South Park Visualisation

A horizonal scrollview to scroll through the 18 South Park seasons.

The selected characters for which the data is visualised. Each character is clickable, the subsequent visualisation can be found in Character Info.

The excluded characters for which the data is not visualised. These can be added to the visualisation by clicking the plus signs.

An enlargement of the squares selected from the horizontal scrollview. The size of a square corresponds to a character's share in spoken words in an episode. The color of a square corresponds to a character's amount of spoken profanities per one thousand words. Episode numbers are clickable, the subsequent visualisation can be found in Episode Info.

Main View Seasons Info

The main view of the South Park visualisation. This view represents the share of each individual character in spoken words and profanities over successive episodes, selected by the selector in the navigation bar at the top.

A wordle of the most popular profanities spoken by the selected character over all seasons. Words in the wordle are clickable and will be visualised in the adjoining linechart.

A linechart which visualises the selected profanities over all seasons in the wordle.

Detailed View Character Info

A detailed view of a character selected in the main view. It sums up the most used profanities of the character and represents the evolution throughout all seasons.

The title & the description of the selected episode, which can be watched on the South Park studios website by clicking the title.

A wordle of the most popular profanities spoken in the selected episode. Words in the wordle are clickable and will be visualised in the adjoining barchart.

The barchart visualises the script of the selected episode. A color corresponds to one specific character present in the selection in Seasons Info. The script of the selected episode can be read by hovering the barchart. The triangles at the top of the barchart indicate the selected profanities in the adjoining wordle.

Detailed View Episode Info

A detailed view of an episode selected in the main view. It sums up the most used profanities in the episode and visualises the script.
